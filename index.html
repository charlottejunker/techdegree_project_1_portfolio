Hi, I’m Charlotte.

I live in London.

Background

I'm an English teacher at an inner-London secondary school. I tried to learn to code from a book (!) ten years ago and then joined Treehouse last year.

I joined the Techdegree to focus my attention on coding a little more, so I can build my skills. I'm not sure where I'm going with it but I'm enjoying learning more about web development.

At school I run a girls' coding club, using Codecademy. The students are learning the languages they want and I am there to support and champion, rather than teach. We are learning together. I started the club because the stats on women in tech are worrying. And even though the girls say they feel they can do anything they want, the uptake of extra-curricular opportunities, course and mixed-gender clubs is low in our school. Girls still don't seem to think computer science is 'for them'.
